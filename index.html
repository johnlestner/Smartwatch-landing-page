Why This Watch

The main benefit of a smartwatch is convenience. With a smartwatch, you can quickly check the time or see incoming notifications without having to dig your phone out of your pocket. This feature can be extremely helpful when you’re in a meeting or otherwise occupied and don’t want to be disruptive by taking out your phone. And if you're someone who's always on the go, a smartwatch can be a great way to stay connected while keeping your hands free. Another benefit of owning a smart watch is that it can help you stay active and healthy. Many smartwatches track your steps taken and calories burned throughout the day.
